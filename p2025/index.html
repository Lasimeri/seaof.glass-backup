Project 2025 Implementation Status

March 2026

Overall: 53% of 532 domestic policy recommendations completed or underway
(Center for Progressive Reform, Feb 2026)

1. Civil Service — Schedule F / "Schedule Policy/Career"

Proposed:

Reclassify ~50,000 "policy-influencing" federal employees as at-will, strippable of civil service protections. Effectively politicize the career bureaucracy.

IMPLEMENTED, UNDER LEGAL CHALLENGE
  • Day 1 EO revived Schedule F, renamed "Schedule Policy/Career"
  • OPM finalized rules Feb 5, 2026 — effective March 8, 2026
  • ~50,000 positions targeted for initial conversion (agencies submitted lists)
  • AFGE, AFSCME, AFL-CIO, Democracy Forward filed amended lawsuit March 5, 2026 in Maryland federal court
  • Unions argue it violates the Civil Service Reform Act, APA, and due process
  • Not yet blocked by a court — litigation is active but no injunction issued as of mid-March

2. DOJ Independence

Proposed:

Subordinate DOJ to presidential control, end post-Watergate independence norms, use DOJ for political enforcement.

SUBSTANTIALLY IMPLEMENTED
  • AG Pam Bondi installed as loyalist
  • FBI Director Kash Patel installed
  • DOJ purged career staff, prosecutors
  • Criminal investigations opened against political adversaries (NY AG, former FBI director)
  • Investigations into Fed Chair Jerome Powell and Fed governors
  • FBI raided Georgia election office (Jan 2026) seeking 2020 election evidence
  • Trump's portrait banner hung over DOJ HQ entrance (Feb 2026)
  • New rule proposed (March 2026) to let AG delay state bar ethics investigations into DOJ lawyers
  • Jan 6 defendants pardoned
  • Half of Americans now doubt DOJ fairness (CSMonitor/polls)
  • No court has reversed the structural politicization — individual actions face scattered challenges

3. Immigration & Border

Proposed:

Mass deportation infrastructure, end TPS, asylum restrictions, local law enforcement deputized for immigration.

SUBSTANTIALLY IMPLEMENTED
  • Expanded ICE cooperation with local law enforcement
  • TPS designations terminated or curtailed
  • USCIS fees increased including asylum application charges
  • Medicaid work requirements imposed (July 2025)
  • Accelerated removals and enforcement priorities
  • Narrowing of humanitarian pathways ongoing
  • Some court challenges on specific TPS terminations and enforcement tactics, but core apparatus is operational

4. Education

Proposed:

Dismantle Department of Education, school vouchers, eliminate "gender ideology" from curricula.

SUBSTANTIALLY IMPLEMENTED
  • K-12 functions announced for transfer to other agencies
  • National school voucher program enacted (July 2025 legislation)
  • Curriculum monitoring for "gender ideology" directed
  • Department restructuring ongoing
  • Title IX transgender protections dropped

5. EPA / Environment / Public Lands

Proposed:

Gut EPA, end climate regulations, expand fossil fuels, roll back endangered species protections, rescind NEPA.

~80% OF PUBLIC LAND PRIORITIES IMPLEMENTED

(Center for Western Priorities)

  • "Unleash American Energy" EO signed Day 1
  • Coal leasing moratorium ended
  • Atlantic, Pacific, Arctic energy leasing reopened
  • BLM quarterly lease sales reprioritized
  • Royalty rates reduced for oil/gas via 2025 legislation
  • All 10 NEPA actions completed or attempted — CEQ NEPA regulations rescinded
  • Ambler Road mining access approved (Oct 2025)
  • 4 ESA rule changes proposed (critical habitat, exclusion criteria)
  • In progress: BLM Public Lands Rule rescission, Roadless Rule rescission, LWCF fund diversion
  • Not yet attempted: Moving BLM HQ west, repealing Antiquities Act
  • BLOCKED: Senator Mike Lee's proposal to sell 3M acres of public land — defeated by bipartisan opposition
  • DOJ legal opinion enabling national monument rescission issued (June 2026 — future action)

6. Reproductive Rights

Proposed:

Defund Planned Parenthood, restrict mifepristone, enforce Comstock Act, reframe abortion as non-healthcare.

~40% IMPLEMENTED, 53% COMPLETED OR IN PROGRESS
  • CDC guidance reframed abortion as non-healthcare
  • EMTALA emergency abortion protections rescinded
  • Biden reproductive health task force revoked
  • Planned Parenthood defunding approved (July 2025 law, Sept 2025 appeals court cleared it)
  • In progress: Mifepristone review by HHS Secretary RFK Jr.; Comstock Act enforcement potential
  • Limited progress on full mifepristone approval reversal

7. LGBTQ+ Rights

Proposed:

Two-gender recognition only, end transgender military service, purge federal LGBTQ+ data, restrict gender-affirming care.

SUBSTANTIALLY IMPLEMENTED
  • Federal websites purged of LGBTQ+ content
  • Gender marker X eliminated from passports
  • $800M+ NIH LGBTQ+ health research halted
  • Transgender military service reversed, gender dysphoria now disqualifying
  • Military DEI programs eliminated
  • Title IX transgender protections dropped
  • NIH ordered to study "regret rates" for gender-affirming care
  • BLOCKED: Federal judge ordered purged webpages reinstated; federal judge blocked cuts to hospitals providing gender-affirming care to trans youth

Key Pattern

The judicial branch has been the primary check, but selectively. Structural changes (DOJ politicization, Schedule F, agency restructuring) have faced less effective resistance than specific discriminatory actions (LGBTQ+ data purges, healthcare cuts) where courts found standing and harm.

Congressional resistance has been minimal except on public land sales.
